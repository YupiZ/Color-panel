Welcome to the ChromaCatcher!

Here's a brief rundown to get you started:

1. Randomize Colors:

Press the "Space" key. Locked colors stay the same.

2. Copy a Color:

Click the color name to copy.

3. Lock Colors:

Click the "Lock" icon next to a color to keep it.

4. Refresh Individual Colors:

Use the "Refresh" button next to a color to change it.

5. Copy All Displayed Colors:

Find all picked colors in the address line! Copy it to save and share the entire palette.
